Meghalaya

Meghalaya is one of the most visited destinations of northeast, because of the diversity of natural aesthetic bounty here. It is considered the hotspot of all things cultural and natural existing in sheer harmony with the constructive human efforts. To date, it remains one of those rare locations that can be visited all year round and still overcompensate every traveller's thirst for a fulfilling retreat
