Madhya Pradesh

Madhya Pradesh – the heart of India – is known for its regal forts, breathtaking topography, exhilarating wildlife, caves, and temples. Perhaps this is why this central state of India is frequented by domestic and foreign travelers round the year. And the vast list of tourist places in Madhya Pradesh contributes all the more to this influx of tourists. There are a lot of places to visit in Madhya Pradesh that satiate the travelers’ instinct. With magnificent temples, rich wildlife, beautiful landscapes and popular cities, Madhya Pradesh is every traveler’s holiday goal
