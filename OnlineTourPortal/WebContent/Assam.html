Assam

Gift-wrapped with rolling fields of aromatic tea, canoes sailing down the Brahmaputra river, or thick jungles with the most bizarre wildlife, Assam is the most charming state in North East India lying in the wait to be explored. Assam never fails to overwhelm wanderers with its beauty and tranquility.
