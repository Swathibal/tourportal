West Bengal

From the majestic Himalayas to the serenity of the Gangetic plains, from quiet seashores to beautiful mangroves, West Bengal is a place with numerous natural wonders. Adding to its appeal, are the vivid festivities, sublime architecture, delightful crafts, classical and contemporary music, and scrumptious ethnic delicacies, that make West Bengal a splendid place offering extraordinary experiences
